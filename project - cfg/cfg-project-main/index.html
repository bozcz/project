Magical winter Holidays in Europe
❮ ❯

For breathtaking views

Enjoy a winter adventure break in beautifully located Tromsø – Norway’s “Northern Lights capital.” Experience exciting activities such as Dog sledding & Aurora Safari, as well as a visit to an Aurora camp.

For winter activities

The winter ski resorts of Europe's Alps and Dolomites are legendary, not only for their superb skiing, but for the postcard Alpine villages and celebrity-studded resort towns at their bases.

For family fun

You can meet Santa Claus at the Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi in Lapland, Finland. Santa spends his time year-round at Santa Claus Village. Feel the Christmas spirit!

There's more to discover

Excited to plan a winter vacation full of joy, adventure and endless magic? From skiing the Swiss Alps to exploring the wilderness of the Arctic Circle, winter is such a special time of year! Where is your winter destination?
